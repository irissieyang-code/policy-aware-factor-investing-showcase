# P2.11 Alt-or-no-SEC tier 1 core manual calibration

This step reviews the five highest-priority issuers from the alt-or-no-SEC routing queue.

## Scope completed in this step

- CCGY | China Clean Energy Inc.
- CRDE | Cardinal Ethanol, LLC
- MEIL | Methes Energies International Ltd.
- REGX | Red Trail Energy, LLC
- XFLS | Xfuels Inc.

## Main takeaways

1. `CRDE` is the strongest alt-core candidate and the only one promoted to a provisional `keep` classification.
2. `REGX` has strong mechanism fit, but it now has a family-link problem because Gevo acquired the Red Trail assets.
3. `CCGY` and `MEIL` still look fuel-relevant in legacy materials, but the evidence base is old and therefore weak for operating-sample use.
4. `XFLS` remains too noisy to treat as a clean operating fuel issuer, but it still belongs in the alt-source watchlist.
